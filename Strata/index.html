NPSO, a super simple responsive site showcasing Nepal.

Nepal is a country located at South Eastern region of Asia.

Being a geographically and culturally rich country, I felt the need to make a exhibition website to showcase this beautiful country. I made this website as a final project of the Web Design specialization. Since, Nepal is so little known in the western world, I felt like if I made a website at least a few people would get to experience this art of nature. :D
